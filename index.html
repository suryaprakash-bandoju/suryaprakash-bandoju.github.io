About Me

My name is Suryaprakash Bandoju, and I'm a final-year B.Tech (EEE) student who slowly discovered a strong interest in the IT world.

My journey wasn't planned — it started with small Python scripts and simple automation experiments, just out of curiosity. The more I explored, the more I enjoyed building things that actually work.

That curiosity pushed me toward Cloud, DevOps, and automation, and soon I found myself learning Linux, Git, cloud services, and building beginner-friendly real-world projects. I enjoy taking an idea, breaking it down, and creating something useful out of it — even if it's small.

Right now, I'm focused on improving my skills, building projects that reflect what I'm learning, and preparing for placements. I may be at the beginning of my journey, but I'm genuinely excited about the path I'm taking and the opportunities ahead of me.

I like learning, experimenting, and growing a little every day — and this portfolio is a small part of that journey.
